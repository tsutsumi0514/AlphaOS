# ADR-0001: Start with a Simple Investment Support OS

## Status
Accepted

## Context
AlphaOS is intended to help an individual investor understand market conditions quickly, reduce information overload, and make decisions with clear reasoning and risk awareness.

## Decision
We will start with a simple MVP that focuses on:
- market summary
- watchlist summary
- risk alerts
- simple UI such as LINE or a minimal web interface

## Consequences
- The first version will be small and easy to maintain
- The product can grow safely over time
- We will avoid auto-trading and unnecessary complexity
