# Codex Guide

This repository is for AlphaOS, an AI-assisted investment support system.

## Rules
- Keep the UI simple
- Reduce information overload
- Show reasoning and confidence
- Prioritize risk management
- Human makes the final decision

## MVP Focus
- Market summary
- Watchlist summary
- Risk alerts
- LINE or simple Web UI

## Do Not
- Build auto-trading
- Add unnecessary complexity
- Show too much data at once
- Hide the AI reasoning
